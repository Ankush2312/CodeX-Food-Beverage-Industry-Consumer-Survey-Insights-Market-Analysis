🥤 Codex Energy Drink – Consumer Insights & Market Analysis (SQL Project)

A SQL-driven business analytics project focused on transforming consumer survey data into actionable business insights for "Codex Energy Drink".

📌 Project Overview
Analysed survey data using SQL to uncover customer preferences, market trends, brand perception, pricing behaviour, and growth opportunities.

🛠 Skills Demonstrated
- SQL Query Writing
- Joins & Aggregations
- Group By & Filtering
- Business Problem Solving
- Consumer & Market Analysis

📊 Key Business Questions Solved
- Which brands dominate the market?
- What factors influence customer purchase decisions?
- Which marketing channels drive the highest reach?
- What pricing strategy should Codex adopt?
- Which cities require stronger market penetration?

🔍 Key Insights
- Brand reputation and taste were major purchase drivers  
- Online advertising delivered maximum customer reach  
- Supermarkets emerged as the primary purchase channel  
- Sports and productivity were top consumption occasions  
- ₹50–₹99 was the preferred price segment  

🚀 Business Recommendations
- Increase digital marketing investment  
- Improve product availability  
- Strengthen brand positioning  
- Focus on fitness and productivity-based campaigns
